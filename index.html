About

Driven by helping others.

A constant throughout my academic journey has been my goal to help other people. Much of my growth has come through volunteer work — tutoring and teaching students from kindergarten through high school — where I learned how differently people interact with their environment based on their unique ways of learning.

I'm drawn to Psychology, Neuroscience, and Marketing alike because I believe the power of helping people can extend in many directions. Through my coursework and my research at the Infant & Child Development Center, I've begun to understand the mechanisms behind human behavior — and through marketing, especially when grounded in that behavior, I see a way to connect with people and extend that impact to a far larger audience.
